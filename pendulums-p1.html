Pendulums Part 1

What are three facts about pendulums?

• Pendulums swing under the influence of gravity
• Pendulums are used to measure the acceleration of gravity.
• Pendulums lose energy due to wind resistance and friction


What are three facts about thermal energy?

•Another name for thermal energy is heat energy
• Thermal energy can be transferred from one object to another
• Thermal energy is created by the acceleration of it's molecule


What are three facts about friction?

•Friction is caused by two surfaces moving in opposite directions
• Friction slows an object down and it eventually stops completely
• Friction generates thermal energy (Heat energy)


What powers a pendulums movement?

Gravity powers a pendulums movement by pushing on the pendulum.


What energy conversion happens when it moves?

When a pendulum moves potential energy is being converted to kinetic energy and kinetic energy is being converted into potential energy


What makes the pendulum stop?

Friction causes the pendulum to stop moving.
